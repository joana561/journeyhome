Croacia

Croatia is a stunning nation surrounding the Adriatic Sea in southeast Europe. It is renowned for its breathtaking coastline, which features more than a thousand islands, pristine waterways, and attractive beaches. The capital of the nation is Zagreb, a city known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and abundance of museums and art galleries. There are eight national parks in Croatia, including the well-known Plitvice Lakes National Park with its tumbling waterfalls and azure lakes, offer beautiful landscapes.

Zagreb

This will be our first stop, we plan to stay in the capital just a single day, we'll arrive in the morning and leave at nigth to Dubrovnik.

We plan to do as much as possible in one day, we want to see the main cathedral, go to Maksimir park, walk around Dolac market and much more.

Dubrovnik

At our second stop, where we will stay for 2 days, we want to feel the Game of Thrones vibe. We will visit the city walls of Dubrovnik, maybe take a ride on the cable car and admire the wonders of the city from above.

Split

From Dubrovnik, we'll head straight to Split, where we plan to visit Suma Marja Park, Diocleciano Palace and St. Dmonio's Cathedral, all in one day. In the next day we plan a trip to Omis Slide, an amazing and adventurous experience over the mountains, before saying goodbye.

Zadar

In Zadar, we will walk along the Venetian city walls, past the Cathedral of St. Anastasia, the Roman Forum, the Church of St. Donato, and much more.

Rijeka

At our last destination in Croatia, the third largest city in the country, we want to enjoy the breathtaking beaches, go to the center where we will find the Rijeka Cathedral as well as the Church of Mary of Trsat,, and finally the Trsat Castle. Then we will proceed our trip to Italy, our next destination.
